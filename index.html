Around Singapore

Merlion and City Skyline

Singapore's national icon, a statue with the head of a lion and the body of a fish. Based on the legend, the fish-like body of the Merlion represents Singapore's origin as a fishing village, while the statue's head represents Singapura, the Sanskrit name for Singapore, which translates to "lion city".

Marina Bay Sands Hotel

Iconic landmark at the heart of Marina. An integrated resort includes hotel, casino, shops, dining and on top is the infinity pool that gives 360o skyline view in city center.

Gardens of the Bay

Home to the largest glass greenhouse in the world, the Flower Dome, this natural park is a popular tourist attraction in Singapore.The flowers are highly sought after for photo ops.

Singapore Flyer

Known as the largest observation wheels in the world, it has 28 air-conditioned capsules providing best view of Marina Bay. One amazing experience is ascending 165 meters to see Singapore's skyline from a vantage point unlike any other.

Jewel@Changi

In this well-known icon in the heart of Changi Airport, explore a fascinating world where you may take in the scenery and go shopping until you drop. It's the ideal location for travelers to stop over because it's easily accessible from every airport terminal.

Universal Studio

One of the reasons tourists choose Singapore is that it is home to the first movie-themed amusement park in Southeast Asia. With 24 rides with movie themes, an interactive museum, and a water park, both adults and kids will have an incredible day.

Wild Wild Wet

The whole family can enjoy a day full of thrills and spills at Wild Wild Wet, one of Singapore's largest water parks. Located in Downtown East, it was even named one of Asia's Top 5 Water Parks.

Mount Faber

The only hilltop location in Singapore with beautiful harbor views and also the home of the Singapore Cable Car.

Sentosa Island

One of the most well-liked locations in Singapore, offering a variety of amenities like water parks, golf courses, hotels, cable cars, and a 2-kilometer beach.

Henderson Wave Bridge

The trail inside this pedestrian bridge is worth exploring for hikers. This Singapore pedestrian bridge is reputed to be the highest in the nation at night. It's beautiful to see, kind of like a giant, bright suspended millipede.

Singapore Zoo

Animal enthusiasts should definitely see this amazing wildlife. The close-up experiences provided, including feeding the animals and viewing the amazing performances in which the animals are the main performers, will appeal to both children and adults.

Jurong Bird Park

The largest bird sanctuary in Asia, home to 5000 kinds of birds, including 24% endangered species. Don't forget to take a picture with the large flamingo flock.

Block750

A place that we call HOME for several years which is filled with love, joy, and wonderful memories. Second home for our loved ones and extended family in Singapore where we frequently had get together to celebrate special occasions.
