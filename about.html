Around Singapore

About Me:

I am Gerry Iglesias, a Filipino citizen. For the past sixteen years, I have lived, worked, and raised a family in Singapore.

For this reason, I have decided to create a photo card grid featuring the locations and attractions in Singapore that my family has visited on a regular basis and that have played a significant role in our lives.

In addition, I want to encourage travelers to put Singapore on their bucket list of destinations and use my photo card as a recommendation for must-see sights to see and do.
